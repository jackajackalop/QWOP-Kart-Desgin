QWOP Kart

by: Yixin He (yixinh)

Like QWOP by Bennett Foddy (which you can play here), but multiplayer racing with Mario-Kart-esque items.

Players race by controlling the thigh and calf muscles of their characters! Interfere with your oponent with items!

Gameplay

Use Q to flex your left thigh! Use W to flex your right thigh! Use P to flex your right calf! Use O to flex your left calf! Press SPACE to use your item! Players can hold only one item at a time, items are placed on the track at random intervals. Items range from minorly beneficial to minorly harmful to players. No new items can be obtained until current (1 item only) inventory is cleared.
